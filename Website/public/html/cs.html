Advanced CS Programming Exercises

At the beginning of the year, we received a set of programming problems to answer. There were a multitude of approaches to solve these problems, some being more efficient than others. For problems involving repetition, I prefer iterative approaches over recursive ones, as they are more resource-efficient in nearly all cases. Towers of Hanoi is the only problem I can think of for which recursion would work better than iteration.
