To review the past tense, we wrote an essay relating to a childhood experience. Proper understanding of the preterite and imperfect tenses, which describe the past in different ways, was integral to this exercise. Verbs conjugated in the preterite tense describe instantaneous actions that occurred in the past, while verbs in the imperfect tense describe habitual or repeated actions. My essay discusses my childhood interest in engineering, how it never came to be, and how it ultimately led to my discovery of computer science.
